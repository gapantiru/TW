1. Intro

PerF is an web application meant to effectively manage imobiliar transaction. The system allows the menagement of some building,apartments,lands for selling/renting. We will also offer information about price,description,coordinates, contact date, and others. We are using a localisation service (Google Map).

2. Creating an account and logging in

In order to have access to our website, you need to create an account..

2.1 How to Create an Account

  1. On the home page, press the button "Register" in the upper-right corner of the page. You will be redirected to the Register page.
  2. On the Register pagell of the information in the form is required, as it is needed to be able to store important data needed for our functionalities.
    Complete the fields carefully, choosing a valid email (if we need to contact you or send notifications to you) and a strong passphrase.
  3. After you have completed filling out the fields, press the Submit! button. This will take you back to the home page. From now on, you will be able to log in.

2.2 How to Log in

  1. In order to login, you have to press the button Login located in the upper right section of the page.
  2. You need to provide the email you registered with and your passphrase.
  3. After that, you can press the button "Login". If you provided the correct information, then the login is successful and you will be redirected to the login page.

3. What Our Website Offers

Through Event-Time, we want to provide anyone that enters our website with the essential information regarding events happening around the world.

This is why we provide users with breaking new events. You do not need to log in or have an account in order to access this information.

3.1 Subscribe to tags

On our website after you register you have to add some tags you're interested in as we have to know what kind of events to show to you.

3.2 Make a request to change an event

As you may browse on out website you may see an event that has some inadequate or incorrect info so if you want an admin to know that there is a problem, you can make a request to change a/some fields regarding that event.

You can find this button on the upper-right side of an event card.

3.3 Export iCalendar

As you may want to have a list of the events your interested in, we got you covered with an Export button, this will make an calendar in iCalendar format and download it.

You can find it in the right-most side of the Upcoming events section.
